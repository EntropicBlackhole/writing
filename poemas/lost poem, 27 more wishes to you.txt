For now, the only wish that I plead to the space and the stars is for you to stay, 
because only you know me from head to toe; 
Only you know that my sadness doesn't get fixed by chocolate (but it does help), 
or that I don't listen to the gossip and I prefer for you to spoil it to me

Or that sometimes, I could be low tide, sometimes I could be a tsunami, 
but either way you're still willing to play along. 
And you know when my words combine with my heartbeats and when they don't.
Or when I break down into whispers and shards, but you keep me up with your love and your hugs

Or when my feet want to follow my doubts, but you know how make me stay; 
You send me notes from your heart, saying "I'm still here"
Only you recognize my giggle from afar. You speak the dialect of my thoughts, 
as if though you understand my eyes fluttering about

You understand me as if we had known each other before, in another place, in another life.
And I'm sure that you know me so well, that you know that my only wish, is for you to stay in my life<3
